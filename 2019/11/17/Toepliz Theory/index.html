什么是Toeplitz定理

Toeplitz 定理具有以下的描述:

设对n,k∈N+n,k \in \mathbb{N}_{+}n,k∈N+​有tnk⩾0t_{nk} \geqslant 0tnk​⩾0, 又成立∑k=1ntnk=1\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk} = 1k=1∑n​tnk​=1, lim⁡n→∞tnk=0\displaystyle\lim_{n \to \infty} t_{nk} = 0n→∞lim​tnk​=0. 若已知lim⁡n→∞an=a\displaystyle\lim_{n \to \infty} a_{n} = an→∞lim​an​=a, 定义xn=∑k=1ntnkak\displaystyle x_n = \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}a_{k}xn​=k=1∑n​tnk​ak​, 则成立lim⁡n→∞xn=a\displaystyle\lim_{n \to \infty} x_{n} = an→∞lim​xn​=a.

尝试估计∣xn−a∣=∣∑k=1ntnkak−a∣⩽∑k=1ntnk∣ak−a∣\displaystyle |x_n - a| = \left|\sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}a_{k} - a\right| \leqslant \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right|∣xn​−a∣=∣∣∣∣∣​k=1∑n​tnk​ak​−a∣∣∣∣∣​⩽k=1∑n​tnk​∣ak​−a∣. 根据这个估计, 我们仅需进行适当的拆分就能解决问题.

对于ε>0\varepsilon > 0ε>0, 总存在N1N_1N1​， 当n>N1n>N_1n>N1​的时候成立∣an−a∣<ε2\displaystyle |a_n - a| < \frac{\varepsilon}{2}∣an​−a∣<2ε​. 于是对∑k=1ntnk∣ak−a∣\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right|k=1∑n​tnk​∣ak​−a∣作拆分为

∑k=1ntnk∣ak−a∣=∑k=1Ntnk∣ak−a∣+∑k=N+1ntnk∣ak−a∣<∑k=1Ntnk∣ak−a∣+ε2.\sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| = \sum_{k=1}^N t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| + \sum_{k=N+1}^n t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| < \sum_{k=1}^N t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| + \frac{\varepsilon}{2}. k=1∑n​tnk​∣ak​−a∣=k=1∑N​tnk​∣ak​−a∣+k=N+1∑n​tnk​∣ak​−a∣<k=1∑N​tnk​∣ak​−a∣+2ε​.

另一方面, 对于同样的ε>0\varepsilon > 0ε>0, 总存在N2N_2N2​， 当n>N2n>N_2n>N2​的时候成立tnk<ε2\displaystyle t_{nk} < \frac{\varepsilon}{2}tnk​<2ε​(k=1,2,⋯ ,Nk = 1,2,\cdots, Nk=1,2,⋯,N); 根据ana_nan​收敛可以知道∣ak−a∣|a_k - a|∣ak​−a∣肯定存在一个上界MMM, 也就是说可以估计为

∑k=1ntnk∣ak−a∣<∑k=1Ntnk∣ak−a∣+ε2<M⋅ε2+ε2=M+12ε.\sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| < \sum_{k=1}^N t_{nk}\left|a_{k}-a\right| + \frac{\varepsilon}{2} < M \cdot \frac{\varepsilon}{2} + \frac{\varepsilon}{2} = \frac{M+1}{2}\varepsilon. k=1∑n​tnk​∣ak​−a∣<k=1∑N​tnk​∣ak​−a∣+2ε​<M⋅2ε​+2ε​=2M+1​ε.

这就完成了定理的证明.

而事实上, 将条件中的“∑k=1ntnk=1\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk} = 1k=1∑n​tnk​=1”改为lim⁡n→∞∑k=1ntnk=1\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk} = 1n→∞lim​k=1∑n​tnk​=1, 命题也是显然成立的.

用Toeplitz定理导出Stolz定理

回忆一下解决数列极限的一大利器 Stolz 定理:

设{an}\lbrace a_n \rbrace{an​}是严格单调增加的无穷大量, 又存在

lim⁡n→∞bn+1−bnan+1−an=l\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_{n+1}-b_{n}}{a_{n+1}-a_{n}} = l n→∞lim​an+1​−an​bn+1​−bn​​=l

(其中lll为有限数或者±∞\pm\infty±∞), 则有

lim⁡n→∞bnan=l.\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_{n}}{a_{n}} = l. n→∞lim​an​bn​​=l.

我们构想通过构造出

不妨将bkak\displaystyle \frac{b_k}{a_k}ak​bk​​写成bk+1−bkkn+1−ak\displaystyle \frac{b_{k+1} - b_{k}}{k_{n+1}-a_k}kn+1​−ak​bk+1​−bk​​的线性表示, 也就是$$\frac{b_k}{a_k} = \frac{b_{k+1} - b_{k}}{a_{k+1}-a_k} \cdot \frac{a_{k+1}-a_{k}}{a_k},$$我们发现ak+1−akak\displaystyle\frac{a_{k+1}-a_{k}}{a_k}ak​ak+1​−ak​​的分子在求和中具有很好的性质, 但是分母却有些不完美. 所以我们根据 Toeplitz 定理的形式作如下改进.

令tn1=a1an\displaystyle t_{n1} = \frac{a_1}{a_n}tn1​=an​a1​​, tnk=ak−ak−1an\displaystyle t_{nk} = \frac{a_{k} - a_{k-1}}{a_n}tnk​=an​ak​−ak−1​​; 令u1=b1a1\displaystyle u_1 = \frac{b_1}{a_1}u1​=a1​b1​​, un=bn−bn−1an−an−1\displaystyle u_n = \frac{b_{n} - b_{n-1}}{a_n - a_{n-1}}un​=an​−an−1​bn​−bn−1​​. 那么很显然∑k=1ntnk=1\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk} = 1k=1∑n​tnk​=1, 并且

∑k=1ntnkuk=∑k=1nbn−bn−1an=bnan.\sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}u_{k} = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{b_{n} - b_{n-1}}{a_n} = \frac{b_{n}}{a_n}. k=1∑n​tnk​uk​=k=1∑n​an​bn​−bn−1​​=an​bn​​.

而lim⁡n→∞un=lim⁡n→∞bn+1−bnan+1−an\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} u_n = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_{n+1}-b_{n}}{a_{n+1}-a_{n}}n→∞lim​un​=n→∞lim​an+1​−an​bn+1​−bn​​, 所以

lim⁡n→∞bn+1−bnan+1−an=lim⁡n→∞un=lim⁡n→∞∑k=1ntnkuk=lim⁡n→∞bnan.\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_{n+1}-b_{n}}{a_{n+1}-a_{n}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} u_n = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^n t_{nk}u_{k} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_{n}}{a_n}. n→∞lim​an+1​−an​bn+1​−bn​​=n→∞lim​un​=n→∞lim​k=1∑n​tnk​uk​=n→∞lim​an​bn​​.
